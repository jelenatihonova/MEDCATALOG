Hypertension Treatment: What Actually Works in 2026

High blood pressure is one of the most common and underestimated conditions today.

Hypertension often has no symptoms but causes long-term damage.

Why Treatment Matters

Untreated hypertension can lead to serious complications:

  • Heart attack
  • Stroke
  • Kidney damage

Medications

Calcium Channel Blockers

Example: Amlodipine

Treatment depends on individual factors and often requires combination therapy.
